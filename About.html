To support young othletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with oportunitles to learn and practice skills related to the game of soccer, specifically those skills around team cooperation and good sportsmanship.

The Northside Youth Soccer Leogue aspires to develop strong, wello-rounded, and mindful othletes through the building of character, self-discipline, and leadership.


General Information

The Northside Youth Soccer Leogue was established in 1996 to provide athletes residng in Chicago's northside neighborhoods on environment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighborhood NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous leogue members.
